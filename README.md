# Telecom-Churn-Dashboard

1. Title
Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

2. Short Description
This dashboard provides a direct, side-by-side comparison of the total customer base against churned customers. It visually isolates the key attributes driving attrition to support data-driven retention strategies.

3. Features & Highlights
- Comparative Analysis: Instantly contrasts the complete Customer Profile (7,043) with the Churner Profile (1,869).
- Key Churn Drivers Identified: The data clearly shows that churn is highest among:
- Customers on Month-to-Month contracts (88.55% of churners)
- Newer customers with low tenure (69% of churners left in the 0-20 bracket)
- Users with Fiber Optic internet (69% of churners)
- Financial Impact: Churners had a higher average monthly charge ($74.44) than the average customer ($64.76), indicating the loss of high-value accounts early in their lifecycle.
- At a Glance Segmentation: Details key differences across demographics, contract types, payment methods, and services to pinpoint high-risk segments.
